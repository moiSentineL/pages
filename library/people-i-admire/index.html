People I Admire

I am made of them.

Alexander Zverev

Incredibly inspirational story.
He was diagnosed with Type I Diabetes as a kid, that couldv’e stopped him, but he didn’t. Several Opens, 4 Grand Slam Finals, 26 years. But finally he won.

Don’t let the world control you. No ailment, no disorder. You cannot be stopped.

Richard Feynman

For letting curiosity take the wheels. And living a damn life.
For making all of this seem so easy.

My mother, Swapna Sikha Choudhury

The strongest woman I know. She’s full of unwaivering conviction and dedication. And of rebellion. Of intelligence. Cunning.

She cannot be stopped either.

Friedrich Nietzsche

Man ahead of his times. Rejected, diminished, manipulated, yet, he remains the icon of many fields. The man who called out. The man who, perhaps, knew.

David Goggins

For owning upto one’s life and being responsible for it, and teaching that. For excelling in athleticism, and mental strength.

Masochistic, but admirable.

Satvik Anand

My friend with ambitions that don’t stop soaring high. My Cato The Younger, who taught me how to be more patient, how to be a better friend and how not to worry.

Seneca

For documenting timeless wisdom. For being humble, kind and wise.
