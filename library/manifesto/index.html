Manifesto

My guiding philosophy.

This is my Manifesto of Living.

  • Delay gratification.
  • Happiness comes from deep, long, focused, and single-tasked sessions, and not shallow drifting. Deep work is what matters.
  • Only a thought we’ve lived has any value.
  • To live without distractions, to stop and notice.
  • To pick the simplest solution and discard the rest.
  • High effort should lead to high yields, else we don’t do it.
  • Embrace boredom.
  • Be. A state of being cognition gives you real happiness.

(December, 2025)
