Kangaroo

[poem] desires

I want to be a kangaroo.
Hopping over cars
And train stations
And domestic airports
And cities.
Trampling over humans.
Crushing them,
Squishing them.
Hopping on,
With a free mind,
A free spirit.
How ecstatic.
