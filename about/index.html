Who am I, you ask?

I am an experimenter.

I am a fluttering stream of consciousness, jumping about trying to make sense of the world. I’ve removed all the labels I assigned to myself. Now I just see what works, what sticks, and what counts.

Trying to give a shit in the world that doesn’t give a shit, to do some great work, to teach people.

Trying not to drown in the incessant drone of banality.

Things I usually like:

  • FOSS
  • Analog systems
  • Reading
  • Creation
  • Problem-solving.
  • Shifting paradigms (sometimes).
